☕ French Press Coffee Guide
   Hot French Press (1 cup, ~200ml)
 Coffee: 2 tablespoons (≈14g, coarse grind)
 Hot water: 7/8 cup (≈210ml, just boiled, wait 30 sec)
Steps
1. Put coffee in French press.
2. Add 7/8 cup hot water.
3. Put the lid on (do not press yet), wait 4 minutes.
4. Slowly press down, pour.
5. Optional: add milk, soy milk, or coconut milk.

❄️ Cold Brew (1 cup, ~250ml)
 Coffee: 3 tablespoons (≈20g, very coarse grind)
 Cold water: 1 cup (≈240ml)
Steps
1. Put coffee in jar/French press.
2. Add 1 cup cold water, stir.
3. Put the lid on (do not press yet), fridge 12 hours.
4. Press down or filter, pour over ice.
5. Optional: add milk, soy milk, or coconut milk.
